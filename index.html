Welcome to Carebridge

The research presents a comprehensive healthcare monitoring system that leverages IoT, machine learning, and image processing. The web application frontend is developed using Angular for a responsive user experience, while the backend is powered by Flask and connected to a MySQL database, ensuring efficient data management and real-time patient monitoring.

Early detection

Our system can identify health anomalies, behavioral deviations, and emotional distress at an early stage—before they escalate into critical medical conditions. By leveraging real-time monitoring and machine learning, CareBridge enables timely interventions that improve patient outcomes and reduce the risk of emergencies.

Accurate results

CareBridge uses advanced machine learning algorithms and real-time data processing to deliver highly accurate health insights within seconds. Whether analyzing vital signs, detecting behavioral patterns, or assessing emotional states, the system ensures reliable and precise results to support better healthcare decisions.

Easy to use

CareBridge is designed with a user-friendly interface, making it accessible for patients, caregivers, and healthcare providers—even those with minimal technical experience. The system delivers clear insights, real-time alerts, and personalized recommendations with just a few simple interactions.

INTELLIGENT SYSTEM TO ASSIST HEALTHCARE PROFESSIONALS IN MONITORING PATIENT HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

This research project highlights the growing need for intelligent healthcare solutions, especially in developing nations like Sri Lanka, where healthcare infrastructure often faces resource limitations. With an aging population and the rising prevalence of chronic illnesses, real-time patient monitoring has become essential for effective care delivery.

However, traditional monitoring methods are limited by manual processes, delayed responses, and fragmented systems, making it difficult to detect health issues early. To address this, the project aims to develop CareBridge, an intelligent IoT-enabled system that assists healthcare providers in detecting, predicting, and managing patient health conditions. The system integrates real-time behavioral tracking, emotional state monitoring, dietary analysis, and emergency alert mechanisms—delivering proactive, personalized, and efficient healthcare support.

Literature Review

IoT automation is transforming healthcare by enabling real-time patient monitoring and improving caregiver efficiency. Traditional methods lack continuous behavior tracking, personalized nutrition support, mental health care, and timely emergency detection. Care-Bridge addresses these gaps by combining smart sensors, behavior recognition, and image-based dietary analysis to provide more accurate and integrated patient care.

Research Gap

Current healthcare monitoring systems are largely reactive and lack integration of key elements like real-time behavior tracking, mental health support, and personalized nutrition. Many rely on outdated methods, leading to delayed interventions and limited adaptability to individual patient needs.

Research Problem

Traditional patient monitoring systems are often reactive, fragmented, and lack real-time responsiveness. This leads to delayed interventions, higher healthcare costs, and increased caregiver workload. Existing solutions rarely integrate key aspects like behavior tracking, mental health support, emergency detection, and dietary monitoring into a unified platform. As chronic illnesses and aging populations grow, there is a pressing need for intelligent, IoT-enabled healthcare systems that provide continuous, personalized, and proactive patient care.

Research Objectives

The aim of this system is to provide an intelligent and automated healthcare monitoring solution using IoT and machine learning technologies. CareBridge integrates real-time data from wearable devices, cameras, and sensors to monitor patients’ physical activity, emotional state, dietary habits, and vital signs. By continuously analyzing this data, the system can detect abnormalities, predict health risks, and deliver personalized recommendations. It also ensures rapid emergency response through automated alert systems. The overall goal is to enhance patient safety, reduce caregiver burden, and support proactive healthcare decision-making

Methodology

We developed an IoT-based healthcare monitoring system, Care-Bridge, which integrates advanced sensors and machine learning algorithms to monitor patient behavior in real-time. The system captures data through motion sensors and wearable devices, processes images for dietary analysis, and evaluates mental health status. By combining these data streams, Care-Bridge provides timely insights and personalized interventions to enhance patient care, improve caregiver efficiency, and enable smart emergency responses.
